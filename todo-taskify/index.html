Todo taskify.

A simple todo lists app with beautiful UI & smooth transitions.

Open todo taskify GitHub

FEATURES & INFO

beautiful UI.

Todo taskify is made with translucent design, simplicity & smooth transitions in mind, made to be similar to a native app.

Manage lists.

Todo taskify comes with 3 tabs(i.e. active, done & create) for organizing & creating lists. You can also easily edit, mark as done/active & delete specific lists from their dropdown menu!

Get notified.

Get notifications about your lists at specific time simply by setting the "Notify me" field to a certain date in the list creation/edit page.

Access from anywhere, anytime. (big words)

You can access your lists from any modern browser simply by logging into your account. You can also manage your lists even without a internet connection! (with some limitations)

todo taskify is built using vue 3, firebase & tailwindCSS for the ones wondering :)
